Quick Facts

Name: Asexual
Definition: a person who doesn’t experience sexual attraction to others.

Incorrect stereotypes and Struggles:

  • "All asexual people are disgusted by sex" - That is not true, some asexual people have sex, it just might not be something that they find important in a relationship.
  • "You just havent found the right one" - Asexuality is valid, and has nothing to do with the finding the right person
